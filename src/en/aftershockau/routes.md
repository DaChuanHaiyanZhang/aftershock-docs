# Page Composition

[[toc]]

## Layout
The Layout of the Aftershock page is primarily divided into Header, Main, and Footer as the main components of the project. These are common across all pages, though they are rendered as separate components.

### Header
#### Logo - Click to Navigate to Homepage

![aftershock](/screenshots/ScreenShot_2025-11-25_163541_877.png "aftershock")

#### Menu (Navigation Bar) - Menu Component

Configured in a tree structure in Prismic [Data Source]()

![aftershock](/screenshots/9c0397bd-854b-495b-93d2-5a23a757dbed.png "aftershock")

#### Search - Search Component

Used to search for product links. Clicking navigates to the corresponding product page.

![aftershock](/screenshots/ScreenShot_2025-11-25_164420_805.png "aftershock")

#### Region Switching

Click to switch between websites for different regions. Currently supports Australia and Singapore.

![aftershock](/screenshots/2025-11-25_164556_912.png "aftershock")

#### Contact Us

Upon clicking, a sidebar slides in from the right with options to contact Aftershock personnel and receive prompt feedback.

![aftershock](/screenshots/2025-11-25_164759_510.png "aftershock")

#### User Information

In a non-logged-in state, prompts the user to log in or register.

![aftershock](/screenshots/ScreenShot_2025-11-25_165046_924.png "aftershock")

In a logged-in state, displays user personal information options and Aftershock contact methods.

![aftershock](/screenshots/ScreenShot_2025-11-25_165605_550.png "aftershock")

#### Shopping Cart

Displays the user's shopping cart items and recommended products.

![aftershock](/screenshots/ScreenShot_2025-11-25_171404_546.png "aftershock")

#### Banner

Typically displays store promotions, slogans, etc. Clicking navigates to the corresponding page.

![aftershock](/screenshots/20251125171759_32_41.png "aftershock")

## Footer

The Footer component displays various service pages, including product groups, company information, help documentation, contact details, and social media accounts.

![aftershock](/screenshots/ScreenShot_2025-11-28_173914_746.png "aftershock")
